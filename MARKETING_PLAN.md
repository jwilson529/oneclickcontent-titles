# OneClickContent Marketing Plan

## Goal
Build a real OneClickContent social persona that participates in the WordPress, SEO, publishing, and AI-for-content communities, with direct product promotion limited to about 10 percent of output.

## Positioning
- Free BYO-key AI tools for WordPress publishers.
- Helpful, practical, pro-WordPress, and lightly opinionated.
- AI-positive without sounding hypey or spammy.

## Existing brand accounts
- X: https://x.com/oneclickcontent
- Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/oneclickcontentwp/
- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/company/oneclickcontent/

## Operating model
- 90 percent community participation, education, and relationship building.
- 10 percent direct plugin or product promotion.
- Be visible where WordPress builders, publishers, SEOs, and indie product people already talk.

## Priority channels
1. X for daily mingling and reply-first engagement.
2. LinkedIn for polished authority posts and product-building updates.
3. Facebook for mirrored or lighter repurposed updates.
4. Add Bluesky later if the early cadence works.

## Core content pillars
- WordPress publishing workflow tips.
- Better headlines, summaries, and editorial systems.
- Practical AI use for publishers.
- Build-in-public updates on OneClickContent plugins.
- Smart reactions to WordPress ecosystem conversations.

## Content mix
- 40% replies and community engagement.
- 25% educational posts.
- 15% build-in-public posts.
- 10% ecosystem commentary.
- 10% direct product mentions.

## Automation direction
Automate discovery, drafting, queueing, and reporting first. Keep posting and replies human-reviewed until the voice is proven.

### Good automation candidates
- Monitor target accounts and keywords.
- Collect reply opportunities.
- Draft 2 to 3 response options.
- Queue evergreen educational posts.
- Produce weekly engagement reports.

### Avoid full automation for now
- Direct replies without review.
- Humor, controversy, or nuanced community threads.
- Support troubleshooting in public.
- Hard-sell outreach.
